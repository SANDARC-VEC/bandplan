US AMATEUR POWER LIMITS — FCC 97.313
An amateur station must use the minimum transmitter power necessary to carry out the desired communications. No station may transmit with a transmitter power exceeding 1.5 kW PEP.

Effective: March 5, 2012
Revised: Jan 16, 2026
